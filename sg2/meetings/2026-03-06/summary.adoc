=== 2.7 Terminology Issues Raised

Two new GitHub issues were created as a result of discussion:

==== 2.7.1 Use of "Specification"

Ge Peng raised concern that the document uses the term "specification" in a way that could be confusing to non-technical readers. The document is called a companion _specification_ to WMO-No. 1131 (CDMS Specifications), yet its content defines functional and non-functional _requirements_ — a distinct concept. She noted that a reader without prior knowledge might interpret the document as simply a subset of 1131 rather than understanding it defines what a CDMS must do.

Discussion followed about terminology and the WMO document hierarchy. David clarified that WMO has a formal approval process for Manuals, Guides and Guidelines, and this document would either become an annex to 1131 or a standalone numbered document. Denis noted the distinction (common in France and elsewhere) between functional specifications (from the owner's perspective) and technical specifications (for developers), and suggested that terminology consistent with both WMO and OGC/technical contexts be clarified.

Dwayne observed that this document is more specific than 1131 — it takes the high-level specification further and defines how things should be done.

Ian and Ge Peng were assigned to the new GitHub issue to resolve the terminology before finalising the document.

==== 2.7.2 Station Terminology

Ge Peng asked whether the use of "stations" in the introductory text (referring to management of observations from stations) is intentionally broad or should be more specific. Xavier clarified that "station" was used broadly to encompass fixed stations, buoys, ships and similar, specifically to differentiate from satellite or forecast data.

Denis noted that WIGOS uses related terminology including "platform" and suggested a glossary aligned with WMO definitions would help readers. He offered to review existing WMO term definitions. Dwayne supported keeping the term general. Ian agreed to keep it broad for now but created a GitHub issue to clarify the relationship between station, platform and host, and to ensure alignment with WIGOS terminology. David added relevant definitions from the meeting chat to the issue.

Ian and Denis were assigned to this issue.

==== 2.7.3 Document Audience

Dwayne noted that the document is useful not only for developers but also for climate specialists within met services who use the CDMS to verify it meets their requirements. Ian agreed this should be reflected in the document's description of its intended audience. He had already shared the document with two climate specialists in Jamaica and Belize for this purpose.

=== 2.8 Section Allocation for Document Review

Ian asked team members to identify which sections of the document they wish to focus on for detailed technical review, in addition to reading through the document as a whole. The focus for the team should be technical correctness, though editorial changes are also welcome.

David will update the GitHub issues (which are structured by chapter/section) and assign members once they confirm their preferred sections by e-mail. A copy of the latest version of the document will be sent to all members.

Ge Peng confirmed that review feedback should cover both technical correctness and completeness. Editorial improvements are encouraged but should be addressed before the document is sent to wider groups.
